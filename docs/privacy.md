Privacy Policy for Gasshopper

1. Data Collection

Grasshopper does not collect, transmit, or harvest any personal information or user data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

2. Data Handling and Usage

To function as a tab manager, the extension requires permissions to access your browser tabs. This permission is used strictly to organize, group, or manage your active tabs locally. The extension does not read, analyze, or transmit the content or URLs of your tabs to any external servers.

3. Data Storage

Any settings or configurations related to the extension are stored locally on your device using the browser's native storage API. We do not store any of your data on our own servers or any third-party databases.

4. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect or store any user data, we do not share, sell, or distribute any user data to third parties, advertising networks, or analytics providers.
